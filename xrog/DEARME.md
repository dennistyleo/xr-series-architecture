# 1. Mission — Govern Reliability as an Orbit, Not an Incident

Modern infrastructure collapses not from one mistake  
but from **drifted orbits**:

- operational drift  
- semantic drift  
- temporal drift  
- policy drift  
- cross-jurisdiction drift  
- multi-cloud boundary misalignment  
- agentic AI divergence  
- cascading “message-virus” failures (11-19 class events)  

Incidents are only the **visible 15%**.  
The **85%** remains invisible, drifting quietly across boundaries until collapse.

XROG governs these orbits.

It defines the **fields, forces, boundaries, and stability rules** that prevent drift from crossing into failure.

XROG is the world’s first **Reliability Orbit Governor** —  
a framework to maintain systemic stability in the AI era.

- semantic consistency
